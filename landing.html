The Methodology

From Raw Mash to Refined Spirit

Machine learning workflows are notoriously chaotic. We use a distillery analogy because the ML pipeline perfectly mirrors the creation of a fine spirit.

01

The Raw Mash

Teacher Models

Massive foundation models (like Llama 70B) are powerful but bulky. They hold all the raw knowledge but lack focus and efficiency for specific tasks.

02

Fermentation

Synthetic Generation

We introduce seed questions, and the Teacher model naturally "ferments" them into hundreds of thousands of diverse, complex training rows perfectly aligned to your goals.

03

The Copper Still

Unsloth LoRA Fine-Tuning

We boil down the massive dataset using cutting-edge LoRA infrastructure, evaporating the fluff and extracting only the pure logic into a focused 8B parameter adapter.

04

Cask Aging

The Tasting Room

You cannot serve a spirit blindly. Our dual-chat interface and automated MMLU/HumanEval benchmarks let you "taste" the new student model side-by-side against its teacher.

05

The Bottling

The Cellar / Deployment

The final, highly potent, ultra-efficient Student model is bottled into `.safetensors`. Instantly publish to Hugging Face or deploy to a local vLLM REST API.
