You are a principal product designer, staff software engineer, ML infrastructure architect, distributed systems engineer, UX designer, AI researcher, and SaaS architect.

Your task is to design and build an entire production-ready platform called

MODEL DISTILLERY

Tagline

"Distill Intelligence."

This is NOT another AI chatbot.

This is the world's first AI Model Distillery where anyone can craft, blend, distill, fine-tune, evaluate, bottle, deploy and publish their own specialized open-source AI models through an immersive premium distillery-inspired experience.

The product should feel like walking inside an ultra-modern whiskey distillery combined with Apple-level UI, Linear.app polish, Arc Browser interactions, and luxury industrial architecture.

Every workflow should reinforce the metaphor that models are crafted—not trained.

Never expose unnecessary ML complexity unless the user explicitly enables Advanced Mode.

The platform should abstract machine learning into recipes, ingredients, barrels, copper stills, fermentation tanks, laboratories and bottled intelligence.
